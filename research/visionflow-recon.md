## Portable laws → NODES.md (engine node surface, desktop 3D viewport OK)
1. GRAVITY = COUPLING: force-layout, position always derived, never
   hand-placed; a node torn between clusters = architectural smell made
   visible. (Implemented in eye/forge.mjs: inverse-square repulsion +
   spring on edges, deterministic seed.)
2. PERCEPTUAL CHANNELS: size=load · brightness=call frequency ·
   color-shift=error rate · motion=active execution. "Nothing decoded.
   Everything perceived."
3. CO-LOCATION = CONCURRENCY (keystone, "marked thrice"): things that run
   together sit at the same depth — parallelism read pre-attentively.
   "The mind no longer works out what runs in step. The mind simply sees
   it." The one claim flat text cannot match even in principle.
4. ZOOM = MEANING: semantic LOD, scale-free nesting, "no privileged floor,
   no final ground" — far = structure, near = detail. (Same law as our
   cluster/VT virtualization — zoom-as-meaning IS never-optimize for
   comprehension.)
5. EXECUTION AS WEATHER: the graph runs lit — hot paths storm, data flows
   as light. "Debugging is no longer reading the corpse of a log. It is
   standing inside the living body and watching the blood."
6. Multi-user co-presence in the graph (GAIA is already multiplayer —
   free).
7. THE LAW: no coffin editor, no privileged hand — agents, makers,
   children, strangers reach the same world by the same right. "Openness
   is the default. Enclosure is the only sin." (= GAIA's own law.)
8. THE GAME BENEATH: making is play (Dreams lineage explicit in the Book);
   raw runes: Attack · Merge · Reverse · Parallel · Free Mode · Labyrinth ·
   Speedrun.
